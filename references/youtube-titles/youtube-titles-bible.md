# YouTube Titles Bible

## Core Principles & Psychology

**Primary Psychological Triggers:**
- Curiosity (60% of viral titles)
- Desire (46%)
- Fear (39%)

**Curiosity Gaps:** Introduce a hint but don't give the full payoff. Make viewers ask questions.

## Proven Formulas

| Content Type | Formula | Example |
|-------------|---------|--------|
| Comparison | $X vs $Y Thing | $100 vs $1000 camera |
| Listicle | [Number] Best [Topic] of [Year] | 10 best AI tools 2026 |
| Problem/Solution | [Problem] How to fix in [Time] | Black mold removal in 3 minutes |
| Authority | I [Action] for [Time], here's what happened | I woke up 4am for a month |

## Optimization Techniques

- Focus on ONE keyword per title
- Stay under 55 characters
- Frontload important words
- Question marks increase CTR by 41%
- "Half a million" beats "480K" (19.8% higher CTR)

## A/B Testing

- Test packaging variations (Curiosity vs Desire)
- Monitor CTR in first 24 hours
- If CTR below average, change title
- Use TubeBuddy, VidIQ, or YouTube's native tool

## Real Examples
