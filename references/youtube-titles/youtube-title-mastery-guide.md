# YouTube Title Mastery Guide

## CTR Benchmarks

| CTR Range | Performance |
|-----------|-------------|
| Below 2% | Poor |
| 2-4% | Average |
| 4-7% | Good |
| 7-10% | Excellent |
| Above 10% | Viral potential |

## Three Core Emotional Triggers

### Curiosity (Most Powerful)
Trigger phrases: "The reason why...", "What happens when...", "The truth about..."

### Desire (Promise of Gain)
Trigger phrases: "How I [achieved result]...", "The secret to...", "X ways to [get benefit]..."

### Fear (Urgency & Loss Aversion)
Trigger phrases: "Stop doing this...", "The mistake that's ruining your...", "Don't [action] until you watch this"

## Power Words That Boost CTR

**Curiosity:** Secret, Hidden, Shocking, Truth, Revealed
**Desire:** Ultimate, Proven, Free, Easy, Fast, Guaranteed
**Fear:** Warning, Mistake, Avoid, Never, Dangerous
**Authority:** Expert, Official, Tested, Certified

## Title Formulas

1. **Number + Benefit:** "5 AI Tools That Save 10 Hours Weekly"
2. **Curiosity Gap:** "I Paid a Stranger $50 to Edit My Video. The Result Was Disturbing."
3. **Challenge:** "I Survived 50 Hours Buried Alive"
4. **Comparison:** "$1 vs $1000 Laptop: Which Works Better?"
5. **Contrarian:** "Why Your Acne Cream Isn't Working"
6. **Direct Address:** "If You're 18-21, Please Watch This"
7. **Authority:** "13 Years of Business Advice in 90 Mins"
8. **Transformation:** "How I Went From Broke to $10K/Month"
9. **Question:** "Can Linux Replace Windows in 2026?"
10. **Time-Sensitive:** "Do THIS Before You Launch (In 2026)"

## MrBeast's Title Science

1. Ultra-simple language (monosyllabic preferred)
2. Universal concepts (money, time)
3. Complete sentences
4. High stakes visible

**Formula:** I [Simple Verb] [Big Number] [Extreme Thing]

## Technical Rules

- Ideal length: 50-60 characters
- Primary keyword in first 3 words
- Front-load value
- Odd numbers (3,5,7) outperform even
- Specific numbers more credible than round

## Common Mistakes

1. Being too vague
2. Keyword stuffing
3. ALL CAPS spam
4. Burying the hook
5. Generic clickbait
6. Not matching content
7. Ignoring mobile truncation
8. Same formula every video
