## Question Hooks

Questions trigger curiosity and invite self-reflection. They work because readers mentally answer before clicking.

### Templates & Examples

| Hook Example | Why It Works | Adaptation Tip |
|--------------|--------------|----------------|
| "What if your content generated conversations and sales leads even after business hours?" | Triggers curiosity about untapped potential | Change "content" to your deliverable |
| "Ever send a LinkedIn connection request and never heard back?" | Relates to universal frustration | Swap for any common rejection experience |
| "What's the one thing stopping your pipeline from growing right now?" | Invites professional self-reflection | Replace "pipeline" with audience's key metric |
| "Would you take advice from a consultant who has never worked in your industry?" | Sparks debate, +20% dwell time | Great for credibility/hiring topics |
| "If LinkedIn banned likes tomorrow, how would you measure success?" | Encourages shares, thought-provoking | Use hypothetical platform changes |
| "What if you could double your LinkedIn engagement without posting 3 times a week?" | Teases efficiency gains | Promise more results with less effort |
| "Why is nobody talking about XX in the XX industry?" | Highlights overlooked trends | Position yourself as trend-spotter |
| "Red flags to look for in [insert niche]…" | Risk avoidance appeal | List format performs well |
| "Before you do XX like always… check out XX" | Routine breaker, innovation prompt | Challenge default behaviors |
| "If you're feeling skeptical about XX… try this…" | Addresses doubt directly | Builds confidence in hesitant readers |
| "This is for the [type of person] looking for [result]" | Niche targeting, specific appeal | Ultra-specific = higher relevance |

### Formulas

```
"What if [desirable outcome] without [common sacrifice]?"
"Ever [common frustrating experience]?"
"What's the one thing stopping your [key metric] from [desired state]?"
"Would you [make decision] if [challenging condition]?"
"Why is nobody talking about [overlooked topic]?"
```

## Story Hooks

Stories create emotional connection and build trust. Vulnerability and transformation arcs perform best.

### Templates & Examples

| Hook Example | Why It Works | Adaptation Tip |
|--------------|--------------|----------------|
| "I left [common challenging situation] [time period] ago. My winning strategy is [counterintuitive approach]." | Builds emotional connection through transformation | Share your actual pivot story |
| "When I finished [previous position], I [found myself in an uncomfortable situation]." | Vulnerable appeal, relatable | Works for career transitions |
| "I thought I'd found the perfect [position/project]. But then I [reached specific turning point]." | Relatable failures drive engagement | The "but then" creates tension |
| "[Time period] ago, I [took an unconventional action]." | Pure curiosity driver | Keep the action vague in hook |
| "Today, [Company/Product] is valued at [impressive figure] and [impressive metric]." | Inspirational arc | Contrast with humble beginnings |
| "I was stuck in [problem] for [timeframe]. Until I [turning point]:" | Shares vulnerability to foster trust | The "until" promises resolution |
| "Last week I spoke to a CEO who had spent £20,000 on ads without a single qualified lead." | Draws in with relatable failure narrative | Third-person stories also work |
| "My hands were shaking. I couldn't believe this was happening…" | Builds suspense through emotion | Physical sensations = vivid |
| "I spent an entire week trying XX… here's what I discovered…" | Authentic testing narrative | Time investment = credibility |
| "I couldn't believe [insert person]'s reaction to XX…" | Emotional reactions, human interest | Third-party reactions intrigue |
| "Something that's always bothered me about X…" | Personal gripe, relatable vent | Authenticity resonates |
| "I tried every affordable XX brand out there and here are the results…" | Trial outcomes, informed choices | "So you don't have to" saves time |

### Formulas

```
"I left [situation] [time] ago. My strategy: [approach]."
"I was stuck in [problem] for [time]. Until I [breakthrough]:"
"I thought I'd found the perfect [thing]. But then [twist]."
"[Time] ago, I [unconventional action]. Here's what happened:"
"My hands were [physical sensation]. I couldn't believe [event]…"
"I spent [time] trying [thing]… here's what I discovered…"
```

## Contrarian Hooks

Contrarian hooks challenge conventional wisdom. They position you as a thought leader and spark debate.

### Templates & Examples

| Hook Example | Why It Works | Adaptation Tip |
|--------------|--------------|----------------|
| "Most people cold email prospects. I send handwritten notes and close 3x more deals." | Challenges digital norms | Show your unconventional method + result |
| "'You need to hustle 24/7 to build a business.' I disagree! Here's why that's terrible advice:" | Debates common beliefs | Quote the bad advice, then attack it |
| "No funding. No network. Still built a 7-figure SaaS. Here's my 4-step bootstrap method:" | Debunks VC dependency | Remove perceived requirements |
| "My client generated $2M in revenue in 8 months. Without a single sales call." | Questions traditional sales models | "Without" removes expected barrier |
| "Everyone says content is king. But here's what actually builds audiences:" | Shifts focus, promises real insight | "But here's what actually..." is powerful |
| "More leads is not always the answer to more sales." | Challenges status quo | Simple contrarian statements work |
| "Everything you knew about ___ is 100% WRONG!" | Bold challenge, assumption breaker | Use sparingly—can feel clickbaity |
| "This may be controversial but…" | Debate starter, opinion divider | Signals authenticity |
| "___ type of people… stop scrolling!" | Targeted call, audience filter | Direct address grabs attention |
| "I kept getting [pain point], so I tried this instead!" | Alternative path, solution switch | Personal experimentation angle |
| "Instead of buying this [popular product], I have another recommendation…" | Contrasting options | Challenge popular choices |
| "Everyone's talking about LinkedIn's latest algorithm update." | Leverages FOMO while questioning hype | Ride trending topics contrarily |

### Formulas

```
"Most people [common approach]. I [contrarian approach] and [result]."
"'[Common advice].' I disagree. Here's why:"
"No [expected requirement]. No [expected requirement]. Still [impressive result]."
"Everyone says [conventional wisdom]. But here's what actually [works/matters]:"
"[Impressive result]. Without [expected method]."
"Everything you knew about [topic] is wrong."
```

## Statistic/Number Hooks

Numbers add credibility and promise concrete value. They signal organized, actionable content.

### Templates & Examples

| Hook Example | Why It Works | Adaptation Tip |
|--------------|--------------|----------------|
| "Did you know that 70% of LinkedIn users never engage with posts?" | Uses data for credibility and surprise | Lead with surprising stats |
| "10 years building startups taught me these 5 truths about venture capital:" | Distills long-term insights | [Time] doing [thing] = authority |
| "How to land enterprise clients in 30 days without pitching:" | Promises speed without barriers | Timeframe + removed obstacle |
| "The 3 email templates that book 15 discovery calls in 7 days:" | Measurable, actionable results | Specific numbers > round numbers |
| "5 harsh truths about entrepreneurship nobody talks about:" | Reveals unspoken realities | "Nobody talks about" = insider info |
| "I analyzed 1000 startup failures and found 80% made this same mistake:" | Data-backed warning | Research-based hooks build authority |
| "80% of B2B buyers have already made a decision before talking to sales." | Highlights urgency with facts | Industry stats create urgency |
| "This [niche] hack could save you XX amount of money" | Cost-saving appeal | Financial value is universal |
| "4 things you're NOT doing to improve XX…" | Improvement gaps, optimization | "NOT doing" implies missed opportunity |
| "Get more [value prop] in your day by doing this one thing…" | Efficiency booster | Single-action promises are compelling |
| "This is the mistake every [niche] business owner makes…" | Common pitfall, error avoider | Universal mistakes = high relevance |
| "Here are 2 signs you should start doing XX" | Positive indicators, adoption cues | Signs/signals format performs well |

### Formulas

```
"[X] years doing [activity] taught me these [Y] truths about [topic]:"
"How to [achieve result] in [timeframe] without [barrier]:"
"The [X] [templates/steps/rules] that [specific measurable result]:"
"[X] harsh truths about [topic] nobody talks about:"
"I analyzed [large number] [things] and found [percentage] [insight]:"
"[Surprising statistic]. Here's what it means for you:"
```

## Pain Point Hooks

Pain point hooks validate frustration and promise relief. They work because readers feel understood.

### Templates & Examples

| Hook Example | Why It Works | Adaptation Tip |
|--------------|--------------|----------------|
| "Tired of posting on LinkedIn and getting zero engagement?" | Validates frustration | "Tired of [common struggle]?" |
| "Are your firm's content efforts failing to convert high visibility into meaningful, qualified leads? Here's why." | Directly addresses business challenges | Be specific to your audience's metrics |
| "Stop networking at events. Do this instead:" | Addresses inefficiency | "Stop [common action]. Do this instead:" |
| "This quarter I hit $500K ARR. Here are 3 lessons that changed everything:" | Shares growth pains + milestone | Combine achievement with lessons |
| "Before you hire your first employee, read this:" | Prevents common errors | "Before you [action], read this:" |
| "The brutal truth about venture capital after raising $10M:" | Exposes hidden costs | "The brutal truth about [thing]:" |
| "5 signs you're ready to scale your agency:" | Self-assessment for growth | Signs/readiness format |
| "Feeling overwhelmed by your to-do list?" | Empathizes with professional stress | Simple emotional validation |
| "If you are into [niche] you need to stop doing this…" | Habit correction | Urgent cessation advice |
| "This is why your ___ isn't working…" | Troubleshooting | Diagnose their problem |
| "Here are 2 signs that you should stop doing XX" | Warning signs | Negative indicators prompt action |
| "Life Hack: Try [product] for [pain point]…" | Quick fix format | Simple solution positioning |

### Formulas

```
"Tired of [common frustration]?"
"Stop [ineffective action]. Do this instead:"
"Before you [major decision], read this:"
"The brutal truth about [topic] after [experience]:"
"[X] signs you're ready to [next level action]:"
"This is why your [thing] isn't working…"
"If you're in [niche], you need to stop doing this…"
```
